Refund Policy

Refund given only for:

  1. Tutor misses 2+ classes without 2-hour notice
  2. Wrong curriculum taught
  3. Platform fails 3+ times in a week
  4. Billing error

No refund for:

“Child not interested”, “Found cheaper option”, or missing class without 6-hour notice.

7-Day Rule:

Miss 3 classes in 7 days without 6-hour notice = remaining sessions forfeited.

Refund Calculation:

Total paid − (Sessions attended × hourly rate)
